Welcome to The Obsidian Assembly

The Obsidian Assembly is a collective of seekers — individuals who pursue hidden truths, forgotten histories, and the unseen structures beneath reality. You have arrived at our digital threshold.

What Is The Assembly?

The Assembly emerged during the First Eclipse, when fragments of forbidden knowledge slipped into mortal understanding. Since then, chosen members have preserved and expanded this archive, studying everything from ancient rituals to modern science.

We do not follow one belief. We follow truth — wherever it leads.

Our Purpose

The Assembly exists to gather, guard, and spread knowledge. Deep mathematics, physics, philosophy, arcane theory, psychology, ritual practice — all of it matters. Knowledge is not good or evil. It simply exists, waiting for someone bold enough to claim it.

Your Role

As an Initiate, your task is simple: explore, learn, question everything. The Archive will guide you. The deeper meanings will reveal themselves when you are ready.

Do not rush. Absorb. Interpret. Grow.
